📖 How to Play / እንዴት ይጫወቱ
1
Deposit / ገንዘብ ያስቀምጡ
Add ETB via Telebirr or CBE. Confirmed by admin within 30 min.
2
Select Stake / ድርሻ ምረጡ
Choose 10, 20, 50 or 100 ETB. Higher stake = bigger prize!
3
Pick Cards / ካርዶቻቸውን ምረጡ
Select up to 4 cards from 1-200. 🟡=yours, 🔴=taken. Game starts after 30 sec.
4
Watch Numbers / ቁጥሮቹን ይከታተሉ
Numbers called every 4 seconds. Your card updates live with ⭐.
5
BINGO! / ቢንጎ!
Complete a row, column or diagonal to win! Prize split if multiple winners.
6
Withdraw / ያውጡ
Request withdrawal to Telebirr or CBE. Processed within 24 hours.
